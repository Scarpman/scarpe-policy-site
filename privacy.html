Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026

ScarPe Media Command is a private content planning, review, and publishing dashboard for owned marketing channels.

Information We Use

When connected to TikTok or another platform, the service may use basic account information, account identifiers, and authorization tokens needed to identify the authorized account and publish content approved by the account owner.

TikTok Data

We use TikTok Login only to identify the authorized TikTok account and to upload or publish content approved by the authorized account owner. We do not sell TikTok user data.

Content Data

The service may store draft captions, media prompts, approved content, publishing status, and related operational metadata needed to manage posts.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with connected platforms only as needed to provide the requested publishing or account-management function.

Security

Access tokens and API credentials should be stored securely on the server and should not be shared publicly.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the owner of the ScarPe Media Command dashboard.
